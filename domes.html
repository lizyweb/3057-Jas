PC DOMES HOUSE SPECIAL FEATURES

  1. Self-supporting space-age buildings: No frame required.
  2. High impact resistance: Stronger than steel (> 8000 psi). High weight load capacity: Up to 4000 kg.
  3. High weight load capacity: Up to 4000 kg.
  4. Aero-dynamic curved surface:Reduces wind resistance, capable of withstanding winds of 160 miles per hour or more (including category 5 hurricanes and 0.5g seismic loads earthquakes).
  5. Long-lasting: With a lifespan of up to 50 years, our domes require no painting and are resistant to water, rot, or insect infestation.
